Q&A

Is mining on android phone profitable?

Yes! Smartphones are suitable cause they have low power consumption and high performance. Older phones may not be as powerful, but it still profitable. Our tests showed earnings of 40-130$ per month(with price 50000$/BTC).

What are my download options?

1. Download app from Google play

2. Download app direct from our website

3. Download app from third-party app stores - GetJar

Can I close the app while I mining?

No. You can minimalize and start another app, but don´t remove it from tasks!

How can I withdraw bitcoin?

There are 2 options:

The first option is send mined bitcoin to another bitcoin wallet. Just enter wallet adrress and amount you want to withdraw.

Another option is send bitcoin to bank account.In the upper part of "Wallet" page click to "Cash", enter IBAN number and amount. We´ll exchange BTC with current exchange rate on Binance and then you will get money in dollars on bank account.

I can mine bitcoin on any mobile phone?

You can mine on phone with have android version 4.1 or newer.

However, the amount of mined bitcoin will depend on the mobile performance. Older devices could be slower then new phones.

Can the phone discharge faster?

Definitely. Your phone will discharge faster, because mining is complex process witch runs on your phone while you mine. We recommend leaving older phones connected to the charger.

Does the phone have to be connected to a network?

Yes. Mining is online process, so without WiFi or Mobile network will mining not working.

However, data usage is very low (50kb/day), so you can seamlessly mining on your mobile network.

Also, when your phone lost connection and then reconect, mining will start again automatically.

Is mining age-restricted?

No, mobile bitcoin mining is not age restricted.

How the rewards work?

When you withdraw bitcoin server automatically check if your reward code someone entred to his app. If so, the reward will be added to his app wallet.

The reward is between 30 - 55$ in BTC, depends on your country.

Why does the mining stops for no reason?

1. Check if you disabled app power saving mods.

2. Check if your WiFi options (in advanced setting). Wifi has to be enebled in sleep mode. -> Turn off saving mode.

3. Add MineOnMobile to "never sleep app".

4. If these steps don´t work last option is to "Enable WakeLock" in reward screen on our app. But you can´t press power button. Just set the brightness to sminimum and leave the screen running.
